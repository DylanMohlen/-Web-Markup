Pavarotti

Pavarotti

Pavarotti's Italian Restaurant, based in the city centre of Leiden, offers a family-friendly vibe with unique furniture and vibrant color choices that transport you into a mini Italy. You will notice the small, strategically placed décor around the entire restaurant. With an open-view kitchen, the entire restaurant is filled with the smell of freshly baked pizza and cooked pasta, making for a very cozy atmosphere. The pricing of the food is quite reasonable and the food quality is definitely worth what you pay for. The helpful staff, fast service, and gentle background music set the perfect tone for a relaxed dining experience, making Pavarotti's a cherished spot for a family meal or perhaps something more intimate.

Annies

Annies

Situated in Leiden’s city center, Annie's boasts a prime location overlooking the charming canals. Upon entering, you’ll feel right at home, whether you choose to sit inside or outside, as the welcoming atmosphere remains constant. Annie's is a family-friendly restaurant with a menu offering something for all ages. The cozy and relaxed vibe makes it an inviting place to dine. The food is consistently good, with a quality guarantee every time, and the kitchen is efficient, so you won't have to wait long for your meal. To make your visit smoother, it's a good idea to book in advance.
